Terms & Conditions

Storage & Service

TreadVault provides tire and wheel storage, pickup, return delivery, and related concierge services. Service availability depends on location, schedule, vehicle/tire type, and storage capacity.

Standard Size Policy

Standard pricing applies to passenger vehicles, SUVs, and light trucks up to 24-inch wheels. Oversized, commercial, specialty, unusually heavy, or 26-inch+ sets may require a custom quote.

Customer Responsibility

Customers are responsible for providing accurate contact information, vehicle details, tire/wheel quantity, pickup location, and any special handling requirements.

Condition of Tires/Wheels

TreadVault may document tire/wheel condition at pickup or check-in. Existing damage, excessive wear, unsafe tires, or pre-existing cosmetic issues are not the responsibility of TreadVault.

Scheduling

Pickup and return appointments are subject to availability. Weather, traffic, service volume, or operational delays may affect scheduling.

Payment & Cancellation

Final pricing may vary based on selected package, tire size, pickup distance, and special handling needs. Cancellation or rescheduling policies may apply after appointment confirmation.
