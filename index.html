Terms & Conditions

1. Reservation Requests

Submitting a reservation form does not guarantee immediate pickup or storage availability. A TreadVault concierge specialist will contact the customer to confirm availability, service area, pricing, pickup/drop-off details, and scheduling.

2. Standard Size Policy

Standard pricing applies to passenger vehicles, SUVs, luxury cars, and light trucks with tire/wheel sets up to 24 inches. Oversized sets, 26-inch+ wheels, commercial tires, specialty truck tires, 18-wheeler tires, unusually heavy sets, or non-standard storage requests may require a custom quote.

3. Pickup & Return Delivery

Pickup and return delivery are subject to location, scheduling availability, weather, and service area. Additional fees may apply outside the normal service zone or for special handling requirements.

4. Customer Information

Customers are responsible for providing accurate vehicle, tire, wheel, and contact information. TreadVault may request photos, tire size, quantity, and condition details before confirming pricing.

5. Tire Condition

TreadVault may inspect tires upon intake for visible damage, excessive wear, unsafe condition, or special handling concerns. Existing damage, prior wear, dry rot, punctures, bent rims, cosmetic marks, or tire age are not the responsibility of TreadVault unless directly caused by confirmed mishandling while in TreadVault custody.

6. Payment

Pricing may be billed monthly, seasonally, annually, or by custom quote depending on the package selected. Storage may not begin until payment terms are confirmed. Late, unpaid, or abandoned storage balances may affect release of stored tire sets.

7. Abandoned Property

If a customer fails to respond to repeated contact attempts or fails to pay outstanding balances for an extended period, TreadVault may treat the stored tire set as abandoned according to applicable law and company policy.

8. Liability

TreadVault provides reasonable care while handling and storing customer tire sets. TreadVault is not responsible for manufacturer defects, pre-existing damage, natural tire aging, ordinary wear, or issues unrelated to storage/handling.

9. Policy Updates

TreadVault may update pricing, service areas, policies, and terms as the business grows. Customers will be informed of material changes that affect active reservations or storage agreements.
